three layer dip

Description

This is a MOST delectable recipe and I am psyched to show you how to make it. Follow along and enjoy this super tasty dish which will make you grow big and strong

Wise and powerful ingredients you will need:

  • Hamburger meat
  • Wisdom tooth of frog
  • Three cups of mixed nuts
  • Two blocks of cream cheese

Steps for the tastiest three layer dip you will ever have

  1. Get your hamburger meat ready. Take it out of the freezer and start tenderizing it until your arm gets tired.
  2. With mortar and pestle, crush the frog wisdom tooth into a fine powder. Feel free to use your rested arm.
  3. In a pie plate, combine the hamburger meat and frog dust, spread evenly using your palm, alternating with your backhand.
  4. You're doing great! Unwrap the blocks of cream cheese and smash them together with your hands. Then, use your palm again to spread evenly.
  5. Put in the oven for 20 minutes at 500 degrees. That'll cook it.
  6. Take it out and wait for it to cool. In the meantime, snack on your gathered mixed nuts. That's what they're there for.
  7. Enjoy this classic, rustic American dish!
