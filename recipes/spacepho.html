Space Pho Recipe Pho' You!

Description

This is a quaint recipe originating from a place far, far away. During a typical Space Force excursion on Jupiter, expert Pho cook Jordan Pedersen (Not to be confused with Jordan Peterson) accidentally created the world's first Pho recipe in space, hence the name - Space Pho!

Things you will need, going forward...

  • 8 ounces of loin beef
  • 3 cubes of beef broth of congruent size and shape
  • 5 cups of orange juice. 3 will work if you don't have enough on hand.
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • Angel hair pasta

Let us begin satiating the beast within us...

  1. In a large pot, throw in your angel hair pasta and douse it generously with orange juice. Toss the 3d beef squares in.
  2. Turn the stove-top to "VERY high" and pour the salt and garlic powder in while you stir.
  3. Once the beef has assimilated with the orange juice, you are ready to turn the heat down to "medium"
  4. Set a timer for ten minutes. Get ready to toss the loin beef in
  5. Yep, it's time to do it. Throw the beef in and keep on stirring. Do this for another 20 minutes.
  6. Having wisely followed these instructions to the letter, it's time to enjoy the fruits of your labor!
