One Hour Pho On The Go

Food Instructions

Vietnamese Pho is a staple of Arkansan cuisine and I am excited to share this low effort recipe with you! This is a dish that has been passed from generations of grandmothers to grandfathers and is sure to impress!

The mouth-watering ingredients. Do not eat them individually. It will ruin the recipe.

  • 6 cups of water
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 5 ounces of gummy worms
  • 3 cups of diced onions
  • Soy sauce (to taste)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 8 ounces of corn, preferably canned
  • Pre-cooked rice
  • Cooking oil spray

From "How" to "Wow!"

  1. Rub your hands together and clap. You'll want to hype yourself up for this one.
  2. Do a hand stand for practice. Once you achieve this, wash your hands because it's time to get started.
  3. Spray your frying skillet with the cooking oil and place the beef on it. Cook to your preference, it's beef after all.
  4. Using a spatula or your bare hands, mix the beef with salt and add the onions,corn and rice. Continue stirring until it looks tasty.
  5. In a large pot, transfer your winning beef combination in and pour the water in.
  6. turn your stove-top on "high" and stir, adding soy sauce
  7. After 60 minutes of dedicated stirring, you should be good to go.
  8. Turn off the heat and wait until it's room temperature. Now you can add in the gummy worms.
  9. Try to save some for the rest of us! You won't want to share!
